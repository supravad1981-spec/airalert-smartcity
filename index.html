AirAlert SmartCity

AI-powered Pollution Monitoring & Prediction Platform

Cleaner Air. Smarter Cities.

AirAlert SmartCity combines citizen-uploaded pollution photos, IoT sensor readings, and satellite imagery to detect hidden pollution hotspots. Artificial Intelligence predicts air quality for the next 24 hours and instantly alerts municipal authorities so resources such as water-mist cannons and cleanup crews can be deployed efficiently.

📸 Citizen Reports

Residents upload smoke, dust and waste images that are automatically analysed using AI.

📡 Sensor Data

Live air quality sensors monitor PM2.5, PM10, CO₂ and NO₂ levels.

🛰 Satellite Analysis

Satellite imagery identifies large-scale pollution and dust movement.

🤖 AI Prediction

Machine Learning forecasts pollution spikes up to 24 hours in advance.

🚨 Smart Alerts

Automatic notifications help municipal teams respond faster.

🌍 Cleaner Communities

Data-driven decisions reduce pollution and improve neighbourhood health.
